## 线性渐变
>linear-gradient

文字带下划线

```
        .demo01{
            display: inline;
            padding-bottom: 5px;
            height: 100px;
            line-height: 25px;
            color: #fff;
            background-image: linear-gradient(180deg, transparent, transparent 94%,#0babf5 98%, rgb(232, 221, 238));
            background-size: 1px;
        }
```
